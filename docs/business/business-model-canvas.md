# Business model canvas

## Customer segments

- Solo frontend developers.
- Freelancers.
- Web agencies.
- SaaS/product teams.
- UI/UX designers.
- Document automation users.
- Enterprise teams with internal reconstruction workflows.

## Value propositions

- Convert screenshots into editable HTML/CSS.
- Save time on manual UI reconstruction.
- Generate printable A4 HTML from document screenshots.
- Preview and refine outputs in one workflow.
- Export standalone HTML.
- Mark uncertain OCR values instead of inventing data.
- Support BYOK for cost and privacy control.

## Channels

- SEO pages for screenshot-to-HTML and PNG-to-HTML.
- Product Hunt and Show HN.
- Developer communities.
- Agency outreach.
- LinkedIn/X demo videos.
- Content marketing.
- GitHub/project README.
- API waitlist.

## Customer relationships

- Self-serve free trial.
- In-product upgrade prompts.
- Documentation and examples.
- Email support for paid users.
- Direct support for agency/enterprise.
- Founder-led onboarding for first customers.

## Revenue streams

- Pro subscriptions.
- Team subscriptions.
- Agency subscriptions.
- Usage credit packs.
- API usage.
- Enterprise/private deployment.
- Future marketplace revenue.

## Key resources

- AI prompt and generation pipeline.
- OCR/provider integration.
- Preview/export engine.
- QA dashboard and comparison data.
- Brand and demo examples.
- Billing and credit system.
- Provider failover and rate limiting.

## Key activities

- Improve generation fidelity.
- Maintain provider reliability.
- Build billing and credit management.
- Run customer validation.
- Create demos and content.
- Monitor costs and margins.
- Support paid users.

## Key partners

- Mistral or other AI providers.
- Vercel or hosting provider.
- Upstash/Redis provider.
- Stripe.
- Design/developer communities.
- Agencies and early beta customers.

## Cost structure

- AI OCR and generation.
- Hosting and server functions.
- Storage for temporary screenshots.
- Redis/rate limiting.
- Observability and logging.
- Payment fees.
- Support time.
- Marketing/content.

## Main assumptions to validate

- Users will pay for saved reconstruction time.
- Screenshot-to-HTML is frequent enough for subscription.
- A4/document fidelity is a strong differentiator.
- BYOK improves adoption without destroying paid conversion.
- Credit limits can protect gross margin.
- Agencies have higher willingness to pay than solo users.

## Validation experiments

1. Run 20 real user screenshots and measure time saved.
2. Offer Pro at 29 EUR/month and measure conversion.
3. Offer Agency beta at 199 EUR/month and measure replies.
4. Publish SEO page and track upload conversion.
5. Test BYOK free tier and measure upgrade to history/QA features.
6. Compare A4/document examples against generic AI chat outputs.
